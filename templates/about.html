About Us:


Developer Student Club is a technical club which is for the students, by the students and of the students. In today’s fast-growing world, technology is changing every second.DSC aims to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge for student. It opts to provide students with the resources and opportunities, all while still pursuing their degrees.Developer Students Club focuses on bringing the best out of students who are always eager to learn something new and make them ready for today’s industry.Developer Students Club opts to shape student developers for a better future. It will be open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills.They take on the responsibility to help students learn and empower these students to solve real problems around us.

Growth and enhancement are the vital pillar for a successful club which require togetherness of every individual in a club. This leads to our motto which says:

Come Together
Learn Together
Grow Together



We, as engineers, have a responsibility towards the development of the society. Being a member of DSC, one gets the guidance to fulfill their dreams of a better tomorrow.Developer Students Club opts to shape student developers for a better future.It is intended to be a space for students to learn and collaborate as they solve problems.

Lets join us in building a community of active students.
